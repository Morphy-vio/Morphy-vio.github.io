Drawing

With 12 years of experience, I’ve mastered various art forms, from Western sketching and oil painting to traditional Chinese painting, woodcut prints, and seal carving. Painting serves as both a creative outlet and a way to document life.

Spent a year creating a challenging 1-meter-square Dunhuang painting, now displayed as a signature piece in the middle school art club.

My oil paintings have earned numerous accolades, including:

  1. First Prize in the “National Primary and Secondary School Painting and Calligraphy Competition”
  2. Gold Medal in the “World Peace Painting and Calligraphy Exhibition for Youth” in Paris, France
  3. Gold Medal in the “World Peace Painting and Calligraphy Exhibition for Youth” in Australia

Applied creativity to all aspects of life, using classroom boards and assignments as canvases.

Shared cartoon art on social media, created personalized gifts for friends, and contributed fan art to celebrity support designs. One cartoon character was featured on concert screens, reaching over 50,000 viewers.
